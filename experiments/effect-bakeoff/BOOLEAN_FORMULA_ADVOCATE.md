# Boolean Effect Formula Candidate Advocate

Date: 2026-07-11
Status: Session AI isolated prototype evidence; not a Hum language decision

## Strongest Case

Boolean effect formulas give one algebra to latent callback requirements,
composition, exact handling, and exclusion. In this prototype, union,
intersection, difference, complement, and equivalence are executable rather
than descriptive. The candidate handles all 12 frozen cases and emits all 29
results through the unchanged Session AG contract: 13 positives are accepted
and 16 misuses are rejected.

The attractive property is precision at abstraction boundaries. A library can
state that a wrapper preserves an unknown callable requirement, adds another
exact requirement, removes a handled requirement, or excludes a forbidden
operation. The caller sees an inferred boundary in the ordinary positives;
the algebraic work stays in the checker and library definition.

This is the strongest case for the model, not a selection. Session AK owns
comparison and any recommendation.

## Executable Model

The prototype represents formulas with constants, exact effect labels,
variables, negation, conjunction, and disjunction. Its public candidate
operations are:

```text
union(a, b)        = a OR b
intersection(a,b) = a AND b
complement(a)      = NOT a
difference(a,b)    = a AND NOT b
equivalent(a,b)    = identical complete truth functions
```

Labels retain their exact corpus identity. Variables are allocated
deterministically and normalize to `$f0`, `$f1`, and onward by stable creation
identity; source names have no semantic weight. Labels sort lexically for
canonical identity. Native canonical evidence retains each formula's own
support. Equivalence evaluates both formulas over one shared,
deterministically sorted union of their atom domains, so tautology,
contradiction, absorption, commutative reordering, double negation, and De
Morgan equivalences compare correctly even when one side has semantically
irrelevant atoms.

That normalizer is deliberately bounded to 16 atoms and fails closed above the
bound. It is a prototype oracle for exact Boolean equivalence, not a proposed
production representation. A production checker would need a compact solver
representation and its own independently measured complexity gate.

## Frozen Corpus Result

Every candidate run starts from fresh state twice. The complete canonical
bytes match. The candidate does not read corpus polarity, expected reason,
expected observation, or required blame sites while building or checking its
native semantic program. Tests poison those answer fields and preserve the
native analysis, then independently mutate callable shape, latent requirement,
iteration overlap, stale views, runtime credit, registration lifetime, exact
authority, allocation, ownership transfer, resource lifetime, and use count.
Each mutation changes the derived reason and blame relationship.

The ordinary positives require no caller-written effect annotation in this
prototype. Stored and returned callables retain their latent requirement facts
and produce explicit callable-environment allocation evidence. The registry,
handler factory, memoizer, logging wrapper, and linear-capture cases remain
the actual frozen shapes; no around-call helper, owner table, copy, or
monomorphic substitute is credited.

## Handling And Exclusion

Retry and timeout handling use formula difference. An absent handled
requirement fails closed. The retained requirement in `retry` stays present,
and erasing it produces the one frozen `unhandled_requirement_erased` reason.

The independent exclusion test constructs `true - blocking.operation`, proves
it equivalent to `not blocking.operation`, and proves it is not equivalent to
unrestricted `true`. This is type-model evidence only. It adds no runtime
handler, scheduler, clock, concurrency, or authority.

## Diagnostics

Rejected variants emit one primary model-neutral reason and the complete
frozen relationship sites. The rendered diagnostic describes the violated
callable, requirement, authority, allocation, registration, or ownership
relationship in domain language. Canonical truth vectors and solver terms stay
in candidate-native audit evidence and never replace the user explanation.

The diagnostic snapshots reject raw AST or truth-table dumps in the rendered
message. The frozen result contract independently recomputes the primary count,
required-site coverage, rendered UTF-8 bytes, native-term count, and presence
of a model-neutral repair.

## Authority, Capture, And Ownership

Formula labels describe latent requirements only. They do not create source
authority, operator consent, or operation exercise. The candidate emits those
three exact fact families separately with the frozen origins and routes. It
rejects a returned handler that loses or widens its exact authority.

Boolean formulas do not by themselves prove closure ownership or resource
lifetime. The prototype therefore exposes separate, uncredited ownership
guards for capture transfer, escape, double use, outliving registration, stale
views, and allocation visibility. Closure environments and cache/registry
storage are explicit. Runtime callable environments remain unimplemented and
uncredited.

This separation is a cost, not a defect hidden by the advocate: a production
formula system would still need a sound bridge to Hum's ownership model and
decision 0017 authority facts.

## Associated-Effect Pressure

Stored and returned callable cases exercise an explicit associated-requirement
constraint between the callable identity and its inferred latent requirement.
The harness prices that checker fact separately. A direct adversarial mutation
of the association fails closed.

This proves only that the bounded prototype does not silently drop the
association. It is not a trait system, generic interface design, or evidence
that associated effects will remain simple in a production language.

## Measured Cost

Implementation cost comes only from the harness-owned checked-in module and
manifest inventory. Candidate execution cannot supply or omit source files.
Every result carries the frozen visited-node, generated-fact,
generated-constraint, normalization-step, maximum-live-item, and diagnostic
measurements, and the harness recomputes them.

The deterministic synthetic growth probe uses 12 distinct atoms. It records:

```text
graph nodes:             12
truth assignments:       4096
generated constraints:   11
normalization steps:     24
```

Two executions produce identical measurements and canonical bytes. This is
bounded evidence of cost, not a throughput claim for a production compiler.
The complete truth-function method is exponential and is intentionally priced
as such.

## Honest Limits

The prototype does not prove global principal types, solver scalability,
ownership soundness, authority safety, production diagnostic quality, or
production performance. It implements no Hum syntax, compiler stage, runtime
path, H-code, schema, command, handler, closure value, IO operation,
concurrency, or associated-effect language surface.

The formula candidate's strongest open risk is not expressiveness. It is the
combined checker, normalization, diagnostic, and library-signature burden once
formulas meet a real generic abstraction system. The bounded truth-function
normalizer makes equivalence exact for this exam but is not itself a viable
answer to that production problem.

and capture candidates without granting credit for any unimplemented bridge.
